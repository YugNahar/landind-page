Our Story

Built to simplify hygiene needs.

Founded by Shubham Jain in 2024, MESSCLE OUTPUTS was built with a vision to provide reliable, scalable, and high-quality cleaning solutions for both consumers and businesses.

We are dedicated to creating smart cleaning solutions that help build healthier, safer, and fresher spaces through effective products, third-party manufacturing support, machinery setup guidance, and practical training.

With growing customer trust and strong market presence, MESSCLE OUTPUTS has achieved over ₹12 lakh in cumulative sales to date, reflecting our commitment to quality, reliability, and long-term customer relationships.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains simple: deliver excellence in every drop while building cleaner and healthier environments for everyone.
